Local Storage Library

Click the "Add +" button to add a new book to the library. Click the "Read" button on each book to change its read status. Click the "Delete" button on each book to remove it from the collection.

In this version, your books will be stored in your own device's memory through the data storage capabilities provided by your internet browser. Your books will remain in memory until you clear your browsing data, or until you click the provided "Clear All" button. You can test this feature by making changes and then reloading or closing and reopening this page. To view what is currently stored in your browser's local memory, press F12 to open developer tools, find the "Application" tab (may be hidden in an arrow), expand the "Local Storage" menu on the left panel, and select the URL of this webpage.
