Local Storage Library

In this version, data is stored in your local disk through your browser's Local Storage API. Your browser provides websites a limited amount of local data storage in string format inside the "cookies and other site data" section of your browsing history. To see what is currently stored in your browser's local memory, press F12 to open developer tools, find the "Application" tab (may be hidden in an arrow), expand the "Local Storage" menu on the left panel, and select the URL of this webpage. All data is stored as key-value pairs, where each are of the string datatype.
